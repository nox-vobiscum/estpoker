What is Estimation Poker? Card-based collaborative estimation…

Estimation Poker is a collaborative practice where team members estimate simultaneously and reveal together. This avoids anchoring and surfaces shared understanding.

When do you use it? Before or during sprint planning…
  • Backlog Refinement & Sprint Planning
  • New/unclear requirements
  • Early-phase rough estimation
Benefits Simultaneous voting, better discussion…
  • Avoids bias (simultaneous reveal)
  • Exposes gaps & risks via divergence
  • Builds shared team understanding
What are Story Points? Relative measure for effort/complexity…

Story Points measure size relatively (not in hours): a mix of scope, complexity, uncertainty and risks. Team comparability grows over time.
